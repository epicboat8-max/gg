## 💡 HOW TO USE

1. **Visit any website** with images (manga site, news, etc.)
2. **Click the floating "T" button** in bottom-right corner
3. **Select "Start Scan"** from the menu
4. **Wait 1-3 seconds** for images to process
5. **See translations** overlayed on images

**That's it!** It just works! ✨
